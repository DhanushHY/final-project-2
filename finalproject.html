Our Story

Meet the passionate couple behind Naivedya! In their mid-20s with an entrepreneurial spirit, they started this venture from their home to bring authentic homemade food to your doorstep. With a focus on quality, hygiene, and traditional recipes, they’ve created employment opportunities while serving delicious meals for birthdays, corporate events, and daily dining. Their mission? To make homemade food accessible without compromising on taste or health.
